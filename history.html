Time Detectives - Mary Rose Museum in Plymouth

Multisensory AR experience for Picture This Productions. It allows the user to investigate the sinking of the Mary Rose (King Henry VIII flagship) in July 1545.
The user can play both in the museum thanks to AR markers as well as at home.

Touchtable Project - Plymouth Museum

A touchtable installation at The Box in Plymouth for Pixel Artworks. The users can explore historical landmarks from the city. In the second mode of operation the user can load two different maps and compare them.

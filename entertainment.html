Wisp World is an ornament and AI companion for Apple Vision Pro. It works alongside your other visionOS apps.

The Blue Violet River - AND Festival 2021

An interactive installation for the Abandon Normal Devices Festival 2021. It took place on the Mersey Ferry, augmenting the riverbank and landmarks of Liverpool. Eight custom-built tablet-based viewfinders positioned through the ferry allowed users to explore a fantasy world of creatures and sculptures scattered through the banks of River Mersey.

Somnai

Fantasy VR was one of the installations within the Somnai interactive experience. This was a multisensory multiplayer VR game that utilised VR backpacks, OptiTrack motion capture system, Leap Motion hand tracking, and it was developed on the Unity game engine.

Mission 828 - Burj Khalifa

Mission 828 is a VR experience in which you become a secret agent on a special mission to stop a cyber attack. You have to interact with the environment and use spy equipement in order to succeed. This installation was at the Burj Khalifa's observation deck. The app was developed using Unity3D and 360 3D footage for the last section was recorded on-site via drones.

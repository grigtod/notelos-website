SLS vs Saturn V

The prospect of visiting the moon once again makes us very excited. Please use the above viewers to compare the Space Launch System from the Artemis 1 mission with the Saturn V from the Apollo 11 mission.

Use the side scrolls to rotate the rocket, the scroll at the bottom to expand it and the 3 buttons to change mode. If you hoover over a component you will see a label with it's name. If you press and hold your left mouse button a magnifying glass will appear.
